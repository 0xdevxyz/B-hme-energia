# Keyword Templates – Local Business Germany

## Pattern: [Industry] + [City]
Use these patterns for H1, title, and meta description.

### Elektriker
- Elektriker {city}
- Elektroinstallation {city}
- Elektriker {city} günstig
- Elektriker {city} 24h
- Notfall Elektriker {city}

### Maler
- Maler {city}
- Malerbetrieb {city}
- Malerarbeiten {city}
- Innenausbau {city}
- Fassadenfarbe {city}

### Klempner / Sanitär
- Klempner {city}
- Sanitär {city}
- Rohrreinigung {city}
- Klempner Notdienst {city}
- Badezimmer renovieren {city}

### Heizung
- Heizungsinstallateur {city}
- Heizung {city}
- Heizungswartung {city}
- Wärmepumpe installieren {city}

### Umzug
- Umzugsunternehmen {city}
- Umzugsfirma {city}
- Umzug {city} günstig
- Möbeltransport {city}

## Local Modifier Keywords
Add these to primary keywords for district-level pages:
- [industry] [city] [district]
- [industry] in [district]
- [industry] [district] [zip]

## Long-tail Patterns (FAQ / GEO targets)
- Was kostet [industry] in [city]
- [industry] [city] Erfahrungen
- Bester [industry] [city]
- [industry] [city] Empfehlung
- [industry] [city] Notdienst
